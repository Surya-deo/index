Info

Fused Filament Fabrication, is an additive manufacturing process that belongs to the extrusion family. In FDM,model is built by depositing melted material in a pre-determined path layer-by-layer. The materials used are thermoplastic polymers and come in a filament form. This can help a lot in Rapid Prototyping as it drastically reduces operation cost.

The Challenge

ABS and PC cannot be printed in cheap printers available in the market. ABS shows wrapping and other problems and demand a suitable environment. Where as PC needs more extreme nozzle/heatbed/chamber temperature and few other parameters. The aim is to print flawless ABS and PC Parts with Cheaply fabricated FDM machine

The Solution

Hotend Modifications
Bed Temp
Controlled environment
